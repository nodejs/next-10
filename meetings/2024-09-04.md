* Actions next steps from the survey
  * Put together Highlights
    * 81% more respondents, many thanks!
    * 71% application developers
    * most common use case reported is building APIs, followed by use as tool for front end application
    * Mac and Windows most common platforms for develpment
    * 85% of production deployments were reported at linux, with docker being 30% of that
    * x86 continues to be dominant architecture but arm is growing
  * 60% of respondents get their binaries from a version manager, of that 56% use nvm
  * npm is still largest package manager, followed by pnpm
  * 25% of respondents said they use corepack
  * Technical priorities seem aligned based on responses on what people thought was important
  * 71% of people reported that their code includes ES module syntax
